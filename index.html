Rahul Moorthy

Ph.D. Candidate, Robotic Sensor Networks Lab

Computer Science, University of Minnesota

I am currently a third year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cites. My advisor is Prof. Volkan Isler . I am currently a visiting student at UT-Austin. My research is focused upon representation learning for occlusion-reasoning and computational geometry problems. Specifically, I am working on projects involving polygon reconstruction based on visibility graph using generative AI and language-conditioned occlusion reasoning.

I am also serving as an AI Head Consultant at Dimensionleap, building agentic workflows for the healthcare and entertainment industries.

Short Summary

I completed my Masters in Robotics recently from University of Minnesota in May 2023 and transferred to a Ph.D. program. I hold an undergraduate degree in Computer Science from Pune Institute of Computer Technology, Pune, India. After my undergraduate studies, I worked as a Robotics Perception Engineer at Brane Enterprises from June 2020 to August 2021.
