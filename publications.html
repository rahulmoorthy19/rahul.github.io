Mahesh, Rahul Moorthy, Automated Wheat Stem Rust Detection using Computer Vision, Diss. University of Minnesota, 2023. - Presented as a poster at ASABE 2023. The research focused upon developing a visual attention approach for detecting stem rust disease which causes occurence of small pastules on wheat. The usecase of the research involved high throughput phenotyping and agricultural robot development for environment monitoring.
